01
Senior Capstone · Client: Varian Medical Systems
Proton Therapy Room Mockup

Proton Therapy uses highly precise proton beams to destroy tumor sites. Children (ages 6–13) often struggle to remain still during the 45-minute procedure. My team partnered with Varian to design a scaled-down, functional mockup of their proton therapy room to educate and calm pediatric patients. The final design features two motors for two degrees of freedom, a ball joint for yaw/pitch/roll, a rotating gantry motor, and a linear actuator for nozzle extension — all controllable with a video game controller.

02
ME4508 Computation & Design · ABAQUS · SolidWorks
Basketball Hoop FEA Analysis

Performed finite element analysis to determine the deformation of a basketball rim when simulating an average person dunking. Applied a 5,635 lb load to the front of the rim to simulate a 175 lb person dunking. Compared carbon steel and aluminum, finding carbon steel achieved a factor of safety of 818 — the safer material. All results were verified with hand calculations.
