About Me

Engineer by training,
problem-solver by nature.

Born and raised in the Bay Area, I moved to Boston to pursue my education at Northeastern University, graduating in May 2025 with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and a minor in Mathematics.

I'm currently enrolled in Northeastern's Graduate Certificate program in Data Analytics Engineering, bridging my mechanical background with modern data-driven approaches.

My career interests lie in the medical device and aerospace/defense industries, where precision engineering meets meaningful impact. My passion for engineering was sparked by building with LEGOs as a kid and continued through repairing my own bike in high school.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy soccer, tennis, basketball, hiking, traveling, and challenging myself with new recipes in the kitchen.
